About Us

Our team's roots trace back to the summer of 2009 in Roswell, New Mexico. As a group of four high school best friends living in one of the most haunted places in the United States, we have always had a fascination with the paranormal. We decided to use this opportunity to kickstart our own paranormal vacation company where we can showcase some of the best haunted destinations in the country.

With multiple incredible destinations to choose from, we strive to offer the most hauntingly chilling and thrilling experience for our travelers.

Our Timeline

  • 2009: Our team of high schoolers begin holding club meetings in a garage in the hot New Mexico summer.
  • 2011: We begin our journey as a paranormal team, establishing our Unreal Odysseys team name.
  • 2016: We begin our paranormal travel business under the name Unreal Odysseys Inc., offering local in-state ghost tours and vacations.
  • 2019: We expand our company nationwide, offering multiple incredibly haunted travel destinations coast-to-coast.
